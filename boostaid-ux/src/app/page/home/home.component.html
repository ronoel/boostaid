Our Mission

BoostAid DAO: Future of Community Governance

BoostAid is committed to evolving into a community-driven ecosystem. As part of our future plans, we will be implementing the BoostAid Decentralized Autonomous Organization (DAO), which will empower supporters, project creators, and influencers to actively participate in the platform's governance and shape its direction.

Upcoming Community Governance

The BoostAid DAO will distribute governance rights to the community through BoostAid DAO tokens. These tokens will be claimable based on your level of support for fundraising campaigns on our platform. As a token holder, you will have the power to vote on key aspects of the platform, including:

  • Platform Tax Rates: Propose and adjust the rates to ensure fair and sustainable operations.
  • Commission Structures: Influence how commissions are distributed among participants.
  • New Features: Suggest and vote on the implementation of innovative tools and functionalities.
  • Platform Improvements: Prioritize enhancements to improve user experience and overall performance.

Future Staking and Rewards

BoostAid DAO tokens will not just be for voting. By staking your tokens, you will be able to earn yields in STX, proportional to the taxes and commissions generated by the platform. This staking mechanism will incentivize active participation in the DAO, contributing to the platform's sustainability and growth.
