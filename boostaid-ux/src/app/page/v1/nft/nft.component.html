What is a Fundraiser NFT?

A Fundraiser NFT is essential for project creators who want to launch and manage fundraising campaigns on BoostAid. By purchasing a Fundraiser NFT, you gain access to the following benefits:

  • Verified Legitimacy: The NFT acts as a verification badge, signaling to contributors that your project is genuine and trustworthy.
  • Multiple Campaigns: Holders of the Fundraiser NFT can create and manage multiple fundraising campaigns, enhancing your ability to raise funds over time.
  • Community Trust: Builds trust with supporters by demonstrating a level of commitment and credibility.

What is a Promoter NFT?

A Promoter NFT is designed for influencers who want to promote fundraising campaigns on BoostAid. By purchasing a Promoter NFT, you gain access to the following benefits:

  • Referral Link Generation: Create unique referral links to promote campaigns and track contributions made through your referrals.
  • Earning Commissions: Earn commissions based on the funds raised through your referral links, providing an incentive for active promotion.
  • Community Recognition: Get ranked as a top influencer within the BoostAid community, earning recognition each epoch for your promotional efforts.

What is a Supporter NFT?

A Supporter NFT is a token of appreciation for individuals who participate in and support fundraising campaigns on BoostAid. Here's how you can get one:

  • Participate in Campaigns: When you contribute to a fundraising campaign on BoostAid, you automatically receive a Supporter NFT as proof of your contribution.
  • Proof of Contribution: This NFT verifies your support and involvement in the project.
  • Future Rewards: Holders of Supporter NFTs may receive additional rewards or benefits from the projects they support, enhancing the value of their contribution.
  • Community Engagement: Engage more deeply with the projects and creators you support, fostering a sense of community and impact.
